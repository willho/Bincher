Is "Signals" Common?
Yes - in copy trading, "signals" typically refers to trade alerts from other traders you follow. It's used by platforms like 3Commas, Cornix, and crypto Telegram groups. However, it usually implies the alert itself, not the wallet being watched.

Verdict: "Watchlist" is clearer for wallet monitoring. "Signals" works better if you ever add a feed of trade alerts.
