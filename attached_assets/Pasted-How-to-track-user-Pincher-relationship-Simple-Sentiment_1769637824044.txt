Weighted By Context
Interaction	Weight
Words (compliments, insults)	Low
Actions (followed advice, ignored warning)	High
Outcomes (won together, lost together)	Highest
Time spent (long convos, check-ins)	Medium
What Actually Builds Trust
trust_events
├── event_type (advice_followed, warning_ignored, trade_won, etc.)
├── outcome (positive, negative, neutral)
├── weight (1-10 based on significance)
├── recency_factor (recent matters more)
